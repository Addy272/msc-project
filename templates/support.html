Runbook

Standard operating steps

  1. Fetch data

    From Admin or Dashboard, run “Fetch Latest Data” for your symbol.

  2. Train models

    Trigger training; review RMSE/MAE in dashboard metrics or Admin snapshot.

  3. Make predictions

    Use “Make Prediction” to see RF, LSTM, and Ensemble signals.

  4. Review coverage

    Open Coverage to confirm rows and training timestamps per symbol.

FAQ

Why do I see empty charts?

Fetch data first. If symbols are new, there may be no Yahoo Finance history or news available.

Predictions return errors

Train both models. Missing scaler/model files often mean models were never saved.

How do I reset a symbol?

Admin → Clear Data lets you wipe stock, sentiment, metrics, or all three for a symbol.
